Normalization Highlights

Key points for database-course evaluation.

Paper and Model are separatedAvoids mixing publication metadata with model metadata.
Many-to-many relations use bridge tablesPaperAuthor, AuthorAffiliation, ModelTopic, ModelDataSource.
EvaluationResult is isolatedAllows one model to have many benchmark rows with different metrics and settings.
